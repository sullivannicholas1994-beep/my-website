What is PDLC?

Polymer Dispersed Liquid Crystal (PDLC) is a cutting-edge smart film technology that allows glass to switch from opaque to transparent on demand.

In its resting state, the liquid crystals within the film are randomly oriented, scattering light and creating a frosted, private barrier. When an electrical current is applied, the crystals align perfectly, allowing light to pass through for instant clarity.

This transition happens in less than 100 milliseconds, giving you total control over your environment.

What is more, all of our films attach via direct electrostatic adhesion to the glass, meaning no sticky adhesives, no messy installation, and easy retrofits.

Precision Engineering
