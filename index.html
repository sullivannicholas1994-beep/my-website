WHY SMART FILM?

Privacy on Demand

Switch from transparent to opaque in less than 100ms via remote, switch, or smartphone.

UV Protection

Our films block 99% of harmful UV rays, protecting your skin and preventing interior fading.

Thermal Control

Reduce solar heat gain by up to 40%, significantly lowering energy and cooling costs.

Ease of Installation

Seamlessly integrated into new builds or easily retrofitted to existing glass with no adhesive required.

HD Projection

When in opaque mode, our film doubles as a high-definition rear projection screen for branding or media.

Laser Precision

Custom laser-cut designs allow for logos and patterns to be integrated directly into your smart film.
