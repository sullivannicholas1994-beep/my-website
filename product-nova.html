Nova Standard

The Essential Privacy Solution

Reliable Everyday Privacy

Nova Standard is our core PDLC film offering, designed to provide reliable instant privacy for residential bathrooms, office partitions, and meeting rooms. It offers a perfect balance of performance and affordability.

When switched off, the film becomes an opaque, frosted barrier (Haze 3.2%) that completely blocks visibility while still allowing light to pass through. When switched on, it becomes transparent, opening up the space.

Key Applications:

  • ✔ Residential Bathrooms & Shower Enclosures
  • ✔ Standard Office Meeting Rooms
  • ✔ Interior Glazing Retrofits

Technical Specs:

Parallel Light Transmittance84%
Haze (ON State)3.2%
Viewing Angle140°
Operating Voltage48V / 60V AC
